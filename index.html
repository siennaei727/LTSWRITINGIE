IELTS Writing Task 2 Practice

Question: Some people believe that technology has made our lives more complicated. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Band Score Explanation

  • Band 9: Fully answers question, well-developed ideas, flawless grammar.
  • Band 7: Clear position, good vocabulary, minor errors.
  • Band 6: Addresses task but with limited development and some grammar mistakes.
  • Band 5: Incomplete response, frequent grammar errors.

Writing Advice
