IELTS Writing Task 1

Sample Question:

The chart below shows the percentage of households in Europe with internet access from 2000 to 2020. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features.

Advice:

  • Write an overview paragraph.
  • Compare trends, not individual numbers only.
  • Use academic vocabulary (increase, decline, fluctuate).

IELTS Writing Task 2

Sample Question:

Some people believe that universities should focus only on academic subjects, while others think practical skills are more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Advice:

  • Clear introduction with thesis statement.
  • One idea per paragraph.
  • Support ideas with explanations and examples.

Band Score Explanation

Band 9: Fully addresses task, sophisticated vocabulary, flawless grammar.

Band 7: Clear position, good vocabulary, minor errors.

Band 6: Relevant but limited development, noticeable grammar mistakes.

Improvement Tips:

  • Learn collocations.
  • Improve grammar accuracy.
  • Practice timed writing (40 minutes Task 2).
